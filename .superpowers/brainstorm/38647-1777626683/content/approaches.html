Three approaches for the pre-launch carousel

Same dark/red aesthetic throughout. Launch: Sunday 03.05.2026

A

3-Slide Punchy Teaser

Slide 1: "SOMETHING IS COMING." — date stamp 03.05.2026, nothing else
Slide 2: Name + tagline reveal — ARMANI KATEHANO / STATS HUB / GOES LIVE SUNDAY
Slide 3: CTA — "LINK IN BIO. SUNDAY." with @armanikatehano_b.c


Trade-off: Maximum mystery, minimum friction. Easiest to make. Less storytelling arc.

B

4-Slide Countdown Reveal ⭐ Recommended

Slide 1: "48 HOURS." — shock opener, red accent, nothing else
Slide 2: "YOUR TEAM. YOUR STATS. ONLINE." — tagline + name
Slide 3: Locked feature list — GAME RESULTS / PLAYER STATS / LEADERBOARDS / TEAM ANALYTICS (with lock or dot prefix, "unlocking 03.05")
Slide 4: CTA — "LINK IN BIO. 03.05.2026. SUNDAY."


Trade-off: Strong narrative arc. The "48 hours" hook stops the scroll. Locked features build curiosity without giving everything away.

C

5-Slide Full Build

Slide 1: "IT'S ALMOST HERE." — pure atmosphere
Slide 2: Logo / name reveal
Slide 3: Feature teaser — blurred or redacted stats cards
Slide 4: Date reveal — "GOING LIVE 03.05.2026"
Slide 5: CTA + handle


Trade-off: Most complete story. Slightly more effort and more slides to swipe. Better for audiences who don't know the brand yet.
