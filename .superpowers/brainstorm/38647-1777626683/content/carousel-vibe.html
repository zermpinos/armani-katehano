What energy should the pre-launch carousel have?

This shapes the slide count, copy tone, and visual treatment

A

Mystery Teaser

Minimal, cryptic. "Something is coming." Dark, suspenseful. A few slides, little info — just enough to make people curious. Works best if launch is still 1–2 weeks out.

B

Feature Preview

Show the goods early. Same structure as the existing carousel but with "COMING SOON" framing — gives people a taste of game results, player stats, leaderboards. Builds anticipation through specifics.

C

Countdown / Launch Hype

Bold and loud. "IT'S ALMOST HERE." Includes a launch date or countdown prominently. High energy, big typography. Works best if you have an exact date to announce.
